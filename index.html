Our Story

Lekker Restaurant Group was founded in 2012 in the south of the Netherlands with one mission: to make honest, flavorful, and affordable food accessible to everyone.

With three locations across Maastricht, Eindhoven and Heerlen, we combine the warmth of a local restaurant with the convenience of online ordering.

Our chefs work exclusively with local suppliers and seasonal produce — so every bite makes a difference.
